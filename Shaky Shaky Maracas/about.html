About Us

Shaky Shaky Maracas was founded on August 31st, 2017 by Karson Richardson and Drew Geisert. The company is based in the heart of America: Indianola, Iowa

Karson and Drew are students at Simpson College who are looking for a start in the business world by selling a unique and exquisite product.

What are Shaky Shaky Maracas?

Shaky Shaky Maracas offers a brand new line of custom maracas for your entertainment. Each maraca is crafted to perfection. Our maraca's bright colors make playing the maracas fun for all ages.
